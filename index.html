    • About Leona's

    Leona's beauty and personality inspired the concept of our cat café, where cats looking for a home can give you company while you drink delicious coffee. All our cats are up for adoption.

    Leona's menu

    All the menu items at Leona's are entirely plantbased. We offer a wide range of coffee and tea to go with delicious homemade pastries and sandwiches.
