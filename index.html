    • About us

    This is Leona, Sonia and Ika's friend. Leona's beauty and personality inspired the concept of Leona's cat café, where cats looking for a home can give you company while you drink delicious coffee. All our cats are up for adoption.

    All the menu items at Leona's are entirely plantbased. We offer a wide range of coffee and tea to go with delicious homemade pastries and sandwiches.
