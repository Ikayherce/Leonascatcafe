About Leona's

This is Leona. She pawed her way into our lives and inspired the concept of our cat café, where cats keep you company while you drink delicious coffee, and perhaps work or catch up with a friend. All our cats come from the local cat shelter and are up for adoption.

Leona's menu

Would you like a cattucino, a catte or an americatto? Enjoy your drink with one of our delicious cinnameow rolls. All the menu items at Leona's are entirely plant-based. We offer a wide range of coffee and tea to go with delicious pastries and sandwiches.
